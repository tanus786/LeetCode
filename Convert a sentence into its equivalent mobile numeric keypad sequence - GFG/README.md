# Convert a sentence into its equivalent mobile numeric keypad sequence
## Easy
<div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO"><p><span style="font-size:20px">Given a sentence in the form of a string in uppercase, convert it into its equivalent mobile numeric keypad sequence.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<pre><span style="font-size:18px"><strong>Input:</strong>

<strong>Explanation</strong>: For 'G' press '4' one time.
For 'F' press '3' three times.</span>
